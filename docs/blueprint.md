# **App Name**: NovaNext: E-Commerce Growth Platform

## Core Features:

- Homepage Hero Section: Create a strong hero section with headline, subheading, and clear call-to-action buttons to engage visitors.
- Services Overview: Showcase e-commerce services via interactive cards with professional icons, highlighting core offerings.
- Marketplace Account Automation: Automated tool creation for setting up seller accounts, tax, compliance and onboarding across marketplaces like Amazon, Flipkart and others
- Product Listing Optimizer: Generative AI to create SEO-optimized product titles, descriptions, and backend keywords for improved product visibility. This tool will ingest marketplace data to improve product placement
- Contact Form and Business Details: Implement a contact form with email, phone number, and business hours to facilitate business verification.
- Legal Pages Generation: Auto-generate essential legal pages (Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions, Refund Policy, etc.) suitable for payment-gateway compliance.
- Performance Analytics Dashboard: Custom analytics dashboard to track and visualize key metrics, offering actionable insights into marketplace performance.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep, saturated blue (#1A4B93) to evoke trust, security, and innovation, fitting for enterprise solutions.
- Background color: Very light blue (#F0F5FA), almost white, to create a clean and spacious feel while maintaining hue consistency.
- Accent color: Complementary shade of violet (#673AB7), used strategically to draw attention to key actions and interactive elements.
- Headline font: 'Poppins', a geometric sans-serif, providing a modern and fashionable aesthetic.
- Body font: 'Inter', a grotesque sans-serif known for its clean and neutral appearance, ensuring readability.
- Use minimalist line icons to represent services, emphasizing the clean, modern design approach.
- Maintain a clean, grid-based layout with ample whitespace, creating a professional and trustworthy appearance.
- Subtle micro-animations and scroll effects to enhance user engagement without distracting from the content.
